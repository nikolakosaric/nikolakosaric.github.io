Welcome to NK Motorsports

Your Trusted Mobile Mechanic

When your vehicle needs repairs or maintenance, skip the hassle of a traditional shop. I’m Nick, an experienced diesel mechanic and car enthusiast providing reliable mobile auto repair services wherever you need them. Whether you’re at home, work, or stuck on the side of the road, I bring professional, high-quality service straight to you.

Routine Maintenance

From oil changes and brakes, to ball joints and spark plugs, trust me to keep your vehicle safe and on the road, without getting upcharged for work you don't need done 

Diagnostic & Repair

Don't know what's going on with your car? Don't worry, I've got the tools and knowledge to effectively diagnose and repair your car. And if it's outside my scope, I'll still let you know what I've found and explain it in a way that's easy to understand.

Upgrades & Installs

Want to lower your car, put a better radio in, or add an exhaust? Even if you don't know what parts to get, I can help you pick the right quality parts in your budget and make your car unique and yours!

Get your car fixed today

​With my expertise and commitment to quality, I make car care simple, affordable, and stress-free. No hidden fees, no unnecessary upsells—just honest work to keep your vehicle running its best. Need a mobile mechanic you can count on? Contact me today to schedule a service!
